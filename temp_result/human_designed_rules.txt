rule4: You can collect iron if you have at least one stone_pickaxe in your inventory.

rule7: You can make wood_pickaxe if you have at least one wood in your inventory.
rule8: You can make stone_pickaxe if you have at least one wood and one stone in your inventory.
rule9: You can make iron_pickaxe if you have at least one wood, one coal and one iron in your inventory.
